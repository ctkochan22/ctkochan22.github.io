Thor's Quest

Clue

In this lair there is a shoe. One that is fun to have, but not to wear. It is a shoe only men and women can yield, but only fits a child. The location of this shoe is a place commonly wet and equally dry. But beware your search. There are traps laid by the Ice God. Once you find this shoe, learn the owner of this shoe.
